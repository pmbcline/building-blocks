History of Roller Derby

Why Roller Derby is the Best Sport Ever

And Has Been for the Past 100 Years

Roller derby has been around since the 1920s, when it began as an endurance sport during the Great Depression.

Here are the reasons you should play roller derby:

  • Reason 1: It's fun.
  • Reason 2: It's inclusive.
  • Reason 3: It will challenge you.
  • Reason 4: You'll make lifelong friends.
